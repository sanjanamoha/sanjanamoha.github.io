Introduction – About me

As a Senior Software Engineer with 8 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in embedded systems and robotics. My work spans industries like automotive and medical devices, where I have led innovations in ADAS, AUTOSAR, real-time systems, and smart rehabilitation technologies.

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Computer Information Systems – AI Specialization at Indiana Wesleyan University. I also hold a Master’s in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Texas at Dallas and a Bachelor's from IIT Kanpur.

My career reflects a blend of research and industry, including published papers, a patent filing, and advanced software-hardware integration projects across global teams.

Beyond work, I find joy in painting, gardening, and exploring new destinations. I am also an avid music enthusiast and often attend rock, metal, and electronic concerts. These passions bring balance and creativity to my engineering journey.
