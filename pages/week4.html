AI-based Chatbot

Tools: OpenAI Chatbot

Objective: Create a chatbot that answers questions about group members.

Business impact: Chatbot creation process simplified for all industries to provide automated information assistance

Process: Fed system prompts with basic info and resumes for each member. OpenAI generated a chatbot capable of returning member details.

Value Proposition: Helps analyse group skillsets and suggests potential projects the group could tackle.

AI Chatbot Link

Reflection: Creating a chatbot that answers questions about group members taught me how to structure AI tools for internal team utility. I tailored the chatbot using prompts and resumes so it serves a peer audience wanting quick insights into collaborator strengths. During testing, I refined prompts to improve accuracy based on feedback from teammates who asked unexpected questions. This process reinforced the importance of anticipating user needs and iterating on design for better usability and information access.
