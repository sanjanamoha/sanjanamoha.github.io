AI-based Chatbot

Tools: Song Maker Custom GPT

Objective: To explore how a simple lyrical concept, a love triangle, can be transformed into diverse musical interpretations using AI. The session aimed to create and refine a multi-version song through iterative, creative collaboration with GPT, showcasing how AI can support songwriting without requiring music production skills.

Business impact: Amateur musicians can help accelerate their career

Process: The session began with a classic guitar love ballad prompt, evolving through user-driven iterations such as piano adaptation, minor key transformation, and emotional tone adjustments. GPT dynamically revised lyrics and chords to match each new vision, including a version where the three characters are unaware of each other and a final shift to reggae dancehall style. Each change reflected user input and GPT’s creative flexibility, culminating in distinct yet thematically cohesive songs.

Value Proposition: This session illustrates GPT’s value as a collaborative music creation tool that enables anyone—regardless of technical skill—to explore complex emotional themes, genre shifts, and narrative variations. By generating lyrics, music, and rationale behind artistic choices, GPT empowers users to participate meaningfully in the songwriting process, making music production more accessible, iterative, and creatively fulfilling.

Reflection: This session showed how GPT can support creative expression across styles, even without music training. I explored transformation from guitar ballad to piano, minor key, emotionally uplifted lyrics, and finally a reggae dancehall version, shaping the prompts accordingly. Feedback led me to refine prompt clarity, improving alignment between changes requested and musical output. It emphasizes the value of iterative prompting and adaptive AI collaboration to realize imaginative ideas across mediums.
